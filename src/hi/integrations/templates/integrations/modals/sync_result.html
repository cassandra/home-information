The single end-of-sync surface: always rendered after sync completes, regardless of what changed. Updates and removes are surfaced here in their own right rather than being silently swallowed by an automatic transition to the dispatcher. Renders a hero headline, a stat-card row of change counts, color-coded per-category enumerations of affected entities, and collapsible Errors / Details sections.
